Char Narayan temple

  • Char Narayan, also called Jagannarayan, is perhaps the oldest temple in Patan's Darbar Square.
  • Unlike other temples in the square, it is constructed primarily of brick and was 1 story high with many fine images of stone and an artictic metal statue of Garuda in a stone illar right in front.
  • The temple is completely flattened except for the basal plinth. Some scholars believe it was built in 1565, while others suggest the early 17th century as a more reasonable date.
  • In any case, it honors Narayan, one of Vishnu's manefestations.
